Why We Exist

In today's digital landscape, over two-thirds of organizations have embraced AI and large language models. Yet, a critical challenge persists: many businesses adopt these powerful tools without understanding the risks and implications.

We've observed a concerning trend where employees unknowingly share sensitive business information through public AI platforms, creating significant security and compliance risks. This knowledge gap not only leads to inefficient use of AI but can result in serious legal and business consequences.

At Ozeye, we exist to:

  • Protect your business data while maximizing AI benefits
  • Transform risky AI practices into secure, strategic advantages
  • Build deep understanding of AI capabilities and limitations
  • Ensure compliant and effective AI integration
